Methods of payment and receiving money in fives

First for the buyer

Five service seekers can purchase services through PayPal account, credit cards, or account balances (what is meant here is that sales profits can be used to purchase five-fives services) It is worth noting that the Five Fives account can be charged in advance, and the balance can be used to purchase services later.

Secondly for the seller

The only way Fives allows sellers to pay is through PayPal. But because there are a lot of problems related to Paypal in the Arab countries, there is another way through which you can get your payments in fives, and that is through payment agents. Payment agents are not employees of Fives, but rather they act as a third party that facilitates the payment of sellers in exchange for a specified commission. A payment agent is often a person you can communicate with, and agree with him to withdraw your profits on his personal account on Paypal, and then he transfers the money to you by one of the methods it provides (such as Western Union). Of course, payment agents charge commissions for performing this service, and this commission differs from one agent to another
